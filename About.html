Learning With Us

The Studio Hair & Beauty Training is a private training centre located in the heart of East London. Having first opened its doors in July 2016, the Erika Fernandes has gone from strength to strength seeing its student numbers increase annually since. We strive to be beautician leading provider of hair stylists, beauty therapists, make-up artist, body and skin surgeons and many others.

We also offer a wide range of Level 2 & 3 qualifications through the awarding bodies VTCT and City & Guilds who both offer internationally & UK recognised professional qualifications which are favoured by employers and by studying on a fast-track basis you can be sure you’re on the right path to employment with a salon, spa, cruise liner or even opening your own beauty premises!

All courses are run with small groups of students per tutor, allowing us to provide plenty of physical and theoric attention and personal time to our students and increase the opportunity to ask any questions or seek further support with us.

Our Qualifcations

We have a huge variety of short-term beauty courses

Our short courses allow our students to gain qualified knowledge in a short period of time and get started with their carrers.

For those who are already in the Beauty Market, we also have a large number of courses that will allow you to take you skills to the next level. Most of our courses are fully accredited by ABT (Associated Beauty Therapists), VTCT(Vocational Training Charitable Trust) and GAT (Guild Accredited Training) allowing you to gain insurance to begin working from home or mobile straightaway following your course.
